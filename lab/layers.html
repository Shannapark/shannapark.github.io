CARAMEL MACCHIACTO

Espresso 60ml
Steamed Milk 60ml
Caramel 30ml
Caramel Macchiato. The delicious, multi-layered Macchiato begins with milk steamed until it's stretched and smooth to bring out its natural sweetness and is topped by a dense, creamy foam. Then rich espresso is poured over and through the foam, where it mixes with the milk and creates a brown mark on top.
